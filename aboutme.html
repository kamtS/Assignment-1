About DJ PR

DJ PR is a boutique Melbourne PR firm specialising in assisting Australian tech start-ups make that first big impact. Established in March 2013, our founder and director Dan Johnson, has forever strived to be a worthwhile investment to fledgling businesses. We understand the startup world is lean, nimble, and requires a deft touch. DJ PR achieves all this by becoming the public relations arm of your business.
